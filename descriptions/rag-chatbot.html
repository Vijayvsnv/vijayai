What It Does

A RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) Chatbot connects directly to your documents and knowledge base. Upload your PDFs, manuals, product catalogs or FAQs — and your website visitors can ask any question and get accurate, sourced answers instantly. Unlike regular chatbots, RAG chatbots only answer from YOUR content — no wrong information, no hallucinations.
