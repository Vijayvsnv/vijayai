RAG Chatbot

Let your customers chat with your documents

What it does

A RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) Chatbot lets you upload your PDFs, manuals, product catalogs or knowledge base — and your customers can ask questions and get accurate answers instantly. No hallucinations, no wrong information, fully customizable to your brand.

Key Features

  • Upload PDFs, Word docs, URLs
  • Accurate answers from your content only
  • Zero hallucinations with source citations
  • Embeddable on any website
  • Custom branding and tone
  • Analytics dashboard
