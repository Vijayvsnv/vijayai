Core Features

📞

Random Voice Calls

Get matched with a random English learner instantly. Talk live for up to 30 minutes over high-quality Agora RTC audio. Real conversations, real practice.

📚

Guided Learning Topics

5 progressive levels with 15 topics each — from Icebreakers to Debate Club. Each topic gives you a conversation starter to use in your next call.

🎮

5 Interactive Games

Vocabulary Quiz, Word Scramble, Fill in Blanks, Pronunciation Challenge (voice-checked), and Dictation. Three difficulty levels each, with stars and streaks.

👥

Friends & Direct Calls

Add people you meet as friends, call them directly anytime, and chat with 24-hour ephemeral messages. See who's online with live presence indicators.

📊

Progress Tracking

Track total calls, time spent, call streak, weekly comparison, game accuracy, and game day streak — all in one clean Progress tab.

⭐

Call Rating System

Rate your conversation partner after each call (1–5 stars). Builds community quality and helps the matching algorithm surface better partners over time.

How It Works

1

Have a real conversation. Use topic cards for ideas. Add them as friends after the call.
